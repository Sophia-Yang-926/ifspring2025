Items and Buffs

In R.E.P.O., surviving the hostile environment and extracting valuable items depends on the upgrades and tools at your disposal. Items and buffs enhance your robot’s capabilities, support your team, and help you avoid or outmaneuver threats.

Upgrades

These permanent enhancements improve core stats and allow players to adapt their playstyle:

  • Health Upgrade – Increases your maximum HP by 20.
  • Stamina Upgrade – Gives you 10 more stamina for longer sprints.
  • Sprint Speed Upgrade – Run faster to escape monsters or reach objectives quickly.
  • Strength Upgrade – Carry heavy objects solo, reducing the need for teammates.
  • Range Upgrade – Pick up and interact with objects from farther away.
  • Extra Jump – Gain an additional mid-air jump for evasive maneuvering.

Consumable Items

Useful for short-term survival and in-mission support:

  • Small Health Pack – Heals 25HP.
  • Medium Health Pack – Heals 50HP.
  • Large Health Pack – Heals 100HP.
  • Energy Drinks – Replenish stamina quickly.
  • Traps and Tools – Can temporarily distract or block monsters.

Equipment and Utilities

Advanced items that assist in strategy and combat:

  • Drones – Scout ahead or retrieve loot from dangerous areas.
  • EMP Devices – Disable specific monster types for a short time.
  • Flashlights and Signal Flares – Light up dark areas or signal teammates.

Note: Items can be purchased in safe zones or found during missions. Use them wisely—inventory space is limited!
